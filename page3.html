How does Halo's Multiplayer work?

Halo is an Arena Shooter where everyone gets equal starts, and have to explore the map to pick up weapons or power-ups. The player doesn't have the ability to sprint in the first few installments until Reach where Armor Abilites were introduced. The movement is simple with no other tools to use.

Examples of Game-Modes from Multiplayer

Slayer

4v4 where he objective is to get 50 kills against the opposing team.

Big Team Battle

8v8 slayer on large scale maps with Vehicles.

Infection

Survivors have to fight off against Infected players and survive. The Infected can infect survivors.

Capture The Flag

4v4 where the objective is to capture the opposing team's flag.

SWAT

4v4 where players have no shields and Precision damage is increased.

Oddball

Main objective is to carry the ball for the longest time/gain more points with the ball.

Invasion

6v6 Spartans vs Elites. One team has to attack the defenders and complete objectives.

Assault

4v4 wherethe two teams have to obtain a bomb and plant it in the enemy team's base.
