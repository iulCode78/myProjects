What is Halo?

Halo is a Sci-Fi First-Person Shooter that has been on-going for 19 years. It centers around the character called 'Master Chief', a super soldier who has saved humanity from countless disasters throughout their history from an alien race called the 'Covenant'. There are a total of 10 games in the franchise, excluding the mobile games.

Halo Infinite

The next upcoming Game in the franchise, 'Halo Infinite', is scheduled to release in the Fall Season later this year. It will serve as a launch title for the Xbox Series X, the newest edition of the Xbox Console family. The game will also release on the Xbox One and PC.

Halo Infinite News

Not much has been revealed about the next upcoming title and gameplay is yet to be shown of the game. Recently it was revealed that game will be on Xbox GamePass at launch, which is very appreciated amongst the fans.

Halo Master Chief Collection

Halo Reach is now available for purchase on Steam and Windows through the Master Chief Collection on PC. Recently, Halo: Combat Evolved has been released in the Collection, making die-hard fans and PC players happy.
